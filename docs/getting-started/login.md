# Sign In to Hyver

## Login

1. Go to the Hyver login page
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click **Sign In**

## Forgot Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset code
4. Enter the code and create a new password
5. Sign in with your new password

## Stay Signed In

Hyver remembers your session automatically. If you close your browser and return later, you'll usually stay signed in.

To sign out:
1. Click your **Account Menu** (bottom of the navigation)
2. Click **Sign Out**

## Troubleshooting

### Can't sign in?

- Verify your email address is correct
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Try resetting your password
- Clear your browser cache and try again

### Account not approved?

If you see the Beta Access screen after signing in, your account is still pending approval. You'll receive a notification when approved.
