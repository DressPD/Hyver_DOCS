# Create Your Account

## Sign Up

1. Go to the Hyver landing page
2. Click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Name** - Your display name
   - **Email** - A valid email address
   - **Password** - Must meet security requirements:
     - At least 8 characters
     - Include uppercase and lowercase letters
     - Include at least one number
     - Include at least one special character

4. Click **Create Account**

## Verify Your Email

After signing up, you'll receive a verification email:

1. Check your inbox for an email from Hyver
2. Find the 6-digit verification code
3. Enter the code on the confirmation page
4. Click **Verify**

> **Tip:** Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

## Beta Access

Hyver may require approval before you can access the platform:

- After verifying your email, you may see a **Beta Access** waiting screen
- This means your account is pending approval
- You'll receive a notification when approved
- Once approved, you can access the full platform
