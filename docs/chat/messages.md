# Sending Messages

## Basic Messaging

1. Click in the message box at the bottom of the chat
2. Type your message
3. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button

The AI will begin responding. You'll see the response stream in real-time.

## Stop Generation

If the AI is taking too long or going in the wrong direction:
- Click the **Stop** button that appears during generation
- The AI will stop immediately

## Mentions

You can bring agents, skills, or tools into your conversation:

1. Type `@` anywhere in your message
2. A dropdown appears with available entities
3. Start typing to filter
4. Click or press Enter to select
5. The entity will be highlighted in your message

When you send, the AI will use that entity to help respond.

## Slash Commands

Type `/` to see available commands. These provide quick shortcuts for common actions.

## Tips for Better Responses

### Be Specific
Instead of "Help me with my code", try "Debug this Python function that's raising a TypeError on line 12".

### Provide Context
Include relevant background information, especially for complex tasks.

### Break Down Complex Tasks
For large tasks, start with one step at a time.

### Use the Right Agent
If you're working on a specific domain, mention an agent with that expertise.

## Conversation Continuity

Hyver remembers your conversation history. You can:
- Reference earlier messages ("As I mentioned before...")
- Build on previous responses
- Return to old chats and continue where you left off
