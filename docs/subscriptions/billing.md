# Billing

Manage your payment methods and billing history.

## Accessing Billing

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Billing** or **Billing Portal**
3. Manage your payment details

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Click **Add Payment Method**
2. Enter card details
3. Save

### Supported Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- (Additional methods depending on region)

### Updating Payment

1. Go to billing portal
2. Click on existing payment method
3. Update details or add new method
4. Set default if needed

### Removing Payment

1. Go to billing portal
2. Click on payment method
3. Click **Remove**
4. Confirm

> Note: You need at least one active payment method for paid plans.

## Billing History

View past transactions:

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Find **Billing History** section

Shows:
- Date
- Description (plan, credits, etc.)
- Amount
- Status
- Invoice link

### Download Invoices

1. Find the transaction
2. Click **Invoice** or download icon
3. PDF downloads

## Billing Cycle

### Monthly Plans

- Charged on the same date each month
- If date doesn't exist, charged on last day of month

### Annual Plans

- Charged once per year
- On subscription anniversary

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
- You'll receive email notification
- Hyver will retry automatically
- Update payment method if needed
- Access may be limited until resolved

## Cancellation and Refunds

### Cancellation

- Pro-rated refunds for annual plans (check terms)
- Access continues until period ends
- Can resume before period ends

### Refund Requests

Contact support for refund inquiries:
- Provide transaction details
- Explain situation
- Support will review

## Tips

- Keep payment method current
- Download invoices for records
- Set up email alerts for billing
- Review charges regularly
