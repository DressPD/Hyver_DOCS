# Subscription Plans

Choose the right plan for your usage.

## Accessing Subscriptions

1. Click **Subscriptions** in the navigation (or Account Menu)
2. The Subscriptions page shows your current plan and options

## Available Plans

Hyver offers tiered plans to match your needs:

### Free Tier

- Limited credits per week
- Basic features
- Good for trying Hyver

### Basic

- More credits
- Standard features
- Good for regular users

### Professional

- Generous credits
- Advanced features
- Good for power users

### Enterprise

- Custom credits
- Full features
- Priority support
- Contact sales

## Billing Periods

### Monthly

- Pay each month
- Cancel anytime
- Flexibility

### Annual

- Pay once per year
- Discounted rate
- Best value

## Changing Plans

### Upgrade

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm payment
5. New plan activates immediately

### Downgrade

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select lower plan
4. Change takes effect at next billing cycle

### Cancel

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period ends
5. Can resume anytime before period ends
