# Subscription Plans

Choose the right plan for your usage.

## Accessing Subscriptions

1. Click **Subscriptions** in the navigation (or Account Menu)
2. The Subscriptions page shows your current plan and options

## Available Plans

Hyver offers four tiered plans, each named after an insect:

### Fly (Free)

- **20K credits/week**
- Free forever
- Basic AI models
- Community support

### Bee — $20/month

- **100K credits/week**
- Standard AI models
- Community support

### Wasp — $50/month ⭐ Most Popular

- **300K credits/week**
- Premium AI models
- Priority support
- Priority queue

### Hornet — $200/month

- **2M credits/week**
- Premium AI models
- 24/7 support
- Priority queue
- Dedicated account manager

## Billing Periods

### Monthly

- Pay each month
- Cancel anytime
- Flexibility

### Annual

- Pay once per year (~20% discount)
- Best value

## Changing Plans

### Upgrade

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Upgrade** on the desired plan
3. Confirm payment
4. New plan activates immediately

### Downgrade

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Downgrade**
3. Change takes effect at next billing cycle

### Cancel

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period ends
